Remind Me is best reminder notification application to set reminders and get notification.

FEATURES
- You can set reminder manually as well as through voice commands without physical interaction.
- Remind Me have facility to choose color and icon according to reminder type and your choice.
- You will get on screen notification of particular notification at specific time and date.

USES
- Get active and inactive reminder lists
- You can set medicines reminder with medicine icon and all other reminders of you desired with desired icons.
- Create shopping list and the application give you notification about that.
